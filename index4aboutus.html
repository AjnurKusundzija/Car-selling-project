About Us

Founder: Ajnur Kušundžija

RentACar.AK is a rent a car company and is an integral part of AC Quattro d.o.o. of a domestic group that is an authorized Volkswagen sales and service center in Bosnia and Herzegovina, with more than 25 years of experience in the vehicle market. RentACar.AK offers car rental services of various categories up to premium class vehicles. Our vehicles are mostly new or more recently manufactured, most from the Volkswagen, Audi, Škoda, Seat group. All our vehicles meet Euro 6 standards, equipped with additional equipment, and the possibility of renting a child seat, garmin navigation and WiFi device for mobile internet is also available. In addition to quality vehicles and the high safety and service standards of our vehicles, we offer favorable long-term vehicle rentals, delivery to the client's address in Sarajevo, as well as collection and return of vehicles in our business units Sarajevo Airport and Tuzla Airport.
